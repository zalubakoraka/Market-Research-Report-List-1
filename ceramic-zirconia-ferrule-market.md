<p><p>The ceramic zirconia ferrule market comprises various types, including SC (Subscriber Connector) and FC (Ferrule Connector) ferrules, which are known for their robustness and low insertion loss. ST (Straight Tip) ferrules feature a bayonet-style connector suitable for multimode applications. LC (Lucent Connector) ferrules are compact and popular for high-density connections. Other types include specialty ferrules designed for specific applications. Each type plays a crucial role in optical fiber connectivity, ensuring efficient signal transmission and minimal interference.</p></p>
