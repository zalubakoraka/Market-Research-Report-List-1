<p><p>The electric motorcycle battery market features three primary types: SLI (Starting, Lighting, Ignition) batteries, AGM (Absorbent Glass Mat) batteries, and Lithium batteries. SLI batteries are traditional lead-acid batteries used for starting and powering electrical systems. AGM batteries, also lead-acid, offer improved performance and are spill-proof, making them ideal for various applications. Lithium batteries, known for their lightweight and high energy density, provide longer ranges and faster charging, driving their increasing adoption in electric motorcycles.</p></p>
