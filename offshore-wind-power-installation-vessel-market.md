The Self-Propelled Jack-Up Vessel can move independently and elevate itself above water for stability during turbine installation. Normal Jack-Up Vessels require towage to the site and also elevate to ensure secure operations. Heavy Lift Vessels, designed for transporting and installing large components, use cranes for heavy lifting tasks. Each vessel type plays a crucial role in enhancing the efficiency and safety of offshore wind projects.</p></p>
