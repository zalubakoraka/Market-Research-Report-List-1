<p><p>Circadian Rhythm Sleep Disorders encompass a range of conditions that disrupt the body's natural sleep-wake cycle. Delayed Sleep Phase Disorder (DSP) involves a significant delay in sleep onset and wake times. Jet Lag Disorder results from crossing time zones, affecting sleep patterns. Irregular Sleep-Wake Rhythm features inconsistent sleep times, while Free-Running Type occurs without alignment to the external environment. Shift Work Disorder affects those working non-traditional hours, and Advanced Sleep Phase Disorder (ASP) involves early sleep and wake times, impacting overall health and daily functioning.</p></p>
