<p><p>Sodium-ion batteries for low-voltage applications include three primary types: **Layered Oxide**, **Prussian Blue**, and **Polyanionic Compounds**. **Layered Oxide** materials, such as sodium nickel manganese oxide, offer high energy density and good cycle stability. **Prussian Blue** compounds provide excellent rate capability and low cost, making them suitable for grid storage. **Polyanionic Compounds**, like sodium iron phosphate, emphasize safety and thermal stability. Each type contributes uniquely to the overall efficiency, cost-effectiveness, and performance of sodium-ion battery technology in various applications.</p></p>
