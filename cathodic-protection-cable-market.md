<p><p>Cathodic protection cables are classified into several types based on material composition and application. HMWP (High Molecular Weight Polyethylene) type cables offer excellent durability and resistance to environmental factors. ECTFE (Ethylene Chlorotrifluoroethylene) / HMWP type combines the benefits of ECTFE's chemical resistance with HMWP's strength. PVDF (Polyvinylidene Fluoride) / HMWP type provides superior corrosion resistance, ideal for harsh environments. Other market categories may include alternative materials or specialized cables tailored for specific applications in cathodic protection systems.</p></p>
