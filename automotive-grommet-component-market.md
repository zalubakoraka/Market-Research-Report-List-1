<p><p>The automotive grommet component market is segmented into three primary types based on material: EPDM (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer), NR (Natural Rubber), and SBR (Styrene-Butadiene Rubber). EPDM is known for its excellent weather resistance and durability, making it ideal for outdoor applications. NR offers superior tensile strength and flexibility, suited for various automotive uses. SBR combines performance and cost-effectiveness, commonly utilized in applications requiring good abrasion resistance. Each type caters to specific automotive requirements, ensuring reliability and performance.</p></p>
