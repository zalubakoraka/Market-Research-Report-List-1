<p><ul><li>Rigid Couplings</li><li>Flexible Couplings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive coupling market comprises two main types: rigid couplings and flexible couplings. Rigid couplings are designed to connect shafts in a fixed manner, providing high torque transmission and ensuring precise alignment, ideal for applications with minimal misalignment. In contrast, flexible couplings accommodate various misalignments and vibration dampening, enhancing performance and reducing wear. These couplings are essential in automotive systems to ensure smooth power transmission and reliability, catering to different engineering requirements and operational conditions.</p></p>
