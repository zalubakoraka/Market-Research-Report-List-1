<p><p>The vehicle grade LiDAR scanner market is primarily divided into two types: mechanical LiDAR and solid-state LiDAR. Mechanical LiDAR employs rotating components to scan the environment, providing high-resolution 3D mapping but can be more fragile and bulkier. In contrast, solid-state LiDAR utilizes fixed arrays of laser emitters and sensors, offering a compact, robust design with lower manufacturing costs and reduced maintenance needs. Both technologies serve crucial roles in autonomous vehicles, enhancing navigation and obstacle detection capabilities.</p></p>
