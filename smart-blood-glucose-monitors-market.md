<p><p>The smart blood glucose monitors market is primarily divided into two types: photoelectric and electrode-based monitors. Photoelectric type monitors utilize light to measure glucose levels through non-invasive methods, offering convenience and comfort for users. In contrast, electrode type monitors require a small blood sample and use electrochemical methods to provide accurate glucose readings. Both types cater to the growing demand for diabetes management solutions, enhancing user experience and aiding in effective blood sugar monitoring.</p></p>
