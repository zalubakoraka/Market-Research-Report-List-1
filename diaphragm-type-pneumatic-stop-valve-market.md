<p><p>The diaphragm type pneumatic stop valve market is segmented into three main types: normally closed, normally open, and reciprocating valves. Normally closed valves remain shut until activated, making them ideal for safety and isolation applications. Normally open valves are designed to allow flow until a signal is received, offering quick response times in systems requiring immediate shutoff. Reciprocating valves use a linear motion to control flow, providing precision in applications needing variable flow rates and pressure control.</p></p>
