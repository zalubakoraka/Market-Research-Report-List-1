<p><p>The mineral flocculant market comprises several types, including activated silica, bentonite, metallic hydroxide, and others. Activated silica enhances clarity in water treatment by promoting particle aggregation. Bentonite, a clay mineral, is utilized for its thickening and binding properties, especially in drilling and wastewater applications. Metallic hydroxides, such as aluminum and iron hydroxides, effectively remove impurities from liquids while also aiding in sedimentation. Other types encompass various natural and synthetic flocculants that serve specific functions across different industries.</p></p>
