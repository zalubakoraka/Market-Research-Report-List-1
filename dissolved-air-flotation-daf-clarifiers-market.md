<p><p>Dissolved Air Flotation (DAF) Clarifiers are a water treatment technology that employs the principle of flotation to remove suspended solids, oils, and other contaminants from wastewater. This process involves saturating water with air under pressure, then releasing the pressure, allowing tiny bubbles to form. These bubbles attach to the particles, making them buoyant, and facilitating their rise to the surface where they can be removed as a sludge layer.</p><p>The DAF Clarifiers Market is anticipated to experience significant growth, driven by increasing environmental regulations, the rising demand for efficient wastewater treatment solutions, and expanding industrial applications.
