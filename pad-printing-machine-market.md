<p><p>The pad printing machine market features two primary types: oil pan and oil cup. The oil pan type uses a reservoir that holds ink, allowing for efficient ink transfer and consistent printing quality. This design is suitable for larger runs and varied applications. Conversely, the oil cup type incorporates a smaller ink reservoir that offers more precise control over ink usage and is ideal for detailed work and short production runs. Both types cater to diverse industrial printing needs, enhancing versatility and efficiency.</p></p>
