<p><ul><li>Fixed Table</li><li>Conveyor Table</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital flatbed cutter market is primarily categorized into two types: fixed table and conveyor table. Fixed table cutters feature a stationary platform where materials are placed, allowing precise cutting and engraving for smaller production runs or intricate designs. In contrast, conveyor table cutters utilize a moving belt system to transport materials, enabling faster processing and efficient handling of larger volumes, making them ideal for high-speed production environments. Both types cater to diverse industries, enhancing versatility and productivity in cutting applications.</p></p>
