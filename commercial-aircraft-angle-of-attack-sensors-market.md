<p><p>The commercial aircraft angle of attack sensors market encompasses various sensor types, primarily focused on measuring the angle between the aircraft's wing and the oncoming airflow. Vane angle of attack sensors utilize a movable vane that pivots to indicate the angle, offering precise measurements. In contrast, zero pressure angle of attack sensors use differential pressure measurements to determine the angle without any moving parts. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing flight safety, performance, and efficiency by providing vital aerodynamic data.</p></p>
