Reactive polyamide resins are characterized by their ability to undergo chemical reactions during application, enhancing adhesion and chemical resistance, making them ideal for coatings and adhesives. Non-reactive polyamide resins, on the other hand, do not participate in such reactions, offering stability and durability, often used in casting and laminating applications. Both types serve diverse industries, including automotive, construction, and electronics, catering to specific performance needs.</p></p>
