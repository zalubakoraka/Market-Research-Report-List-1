<p><p>The RTV (room temperature vulcanizing) silicone adhesive market is primarily divided into two types: one-component and two-component systems. One-component RTV silicone adhesives cure when exposed to moisture in the air, offering ease of use and convenience for quick applications. In contrast, two-component RTV silicone adhesives require mixing a base and a hardener before application, providing enhanced strength and durability. Both types cater to diverse industrial uses, from automotive to construction, based on specific bonding and sealing needs.</p></p>
