<p><p>The PEI resin market is divided into two main types: standard PEI resin and transparent PEI resin. Standard PEI resin is known for its high strength, thermal stability, and chemical resistance, making it suitable for engineering applications and high-performance components. In contrast, transparent PEI resin offers similar properties while providing optical clarity, making it ideal for applications such as medical devices and optical components. Both types serve diverse industries, including aerospace, automotive, and electronics, due to their exceptional performance characteristics.</p></p>
