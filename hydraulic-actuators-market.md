<p><p>The hydraulic actuators market is primarily segmented into linear and rotary types. Linear hydraulic actuators convert hydraulic energy into linear motion, commonly used in applications like lifting, pushing, or pulling heavy loads. Conversely, rotary hydraulic actuators produce rotational movement, ideal for tasks such as turning valves or operating machinery components. Both types are critical in industries like manufacturing, automotive, and aerospace, where precise and powerful motion control is essential for enhancing operational efficiency and productivity.</p></p>
