<p><p>Fractional Flow Reserve (FFR) is a diagnostic technique used in cardiology to assess the significance of coronary artery stenosis. This method is instrumental in guiding treatment decisions and improving patient outcomes.</p><p>The Fractional Flow Reserve Market is witnessing significant growth, driven by increasing prevalence of coronary artery disease, innovative technological advancements, and the rising adoption of minimally invasive procedures.
