<p><p>The windshield wiper blades market is divided into two main types: boneless wiper blades and bone wiper blades. Boneless wiper blades feature a sleek, streamlined design without a traditional frame, providing improved aerodynamics and visibility. They are often favored for their aesthetic appeal and quiet operation. Conversely, bone wiper blades, which consist of a metal or plastic frame supporting rubber squeegees, offer durability and reliability in varied weather conditions. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and vehicle requirements.</p></p>
