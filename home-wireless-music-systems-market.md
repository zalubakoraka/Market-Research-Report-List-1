<p><p>Home wireless music systems primarily utilize two market types: WiFi and Bluetooth. WiFi systems offer high-quality audio streaming over larger distances and can connect multiple devices simultaneously, making them ideal for whole-home sound setups. In contrast, Bluetooth systems are known for their portability and ease of use, allowing direct pairing with smartphones and tablets for quick access to music. Both types cater to different consumer preferences, with WiFi focusing on performance and connectivity, while Bluetooth emphasizes convenience and mobility.</p></p>
