<p><p>Healthcare laboratory informatics encompasses five primary market types: </p><p>1. **LIMS (Laboratory Information Management System)**: Manages samples, data, and laboratory workflows.</p><p>2. **ELN (Electronic Laboratory Notebook)**: Provides a digital platform for researchers to document experiments and results.</p><p>3. **SDMS (Scientific Data Management System)**: Organizes and archives scientific data for easy retrieval and analysis.</p><p>4. **CDS (Chromatography Data System)**: Analyzes and manages data from chromatography instruments.</p><p>5. **LES (Laboratory Execution System)**: Guides and tracks laboratory processes and procedures, enhancing efficiency and compliance.</p></p>
