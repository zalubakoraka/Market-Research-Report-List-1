<p><p>The Synchronous Optical Networking (SONET) market includes various types such as Wavelength Division Multiplexing (WDM) and Fibre Channel. WDM enables multiple data signals to be transmitted simultaneously over a single fiber by allocating different wavelengths, enhancing capacity and efficiency. SONET provides a standard for transmitting digital information over optical networks, ensuring compatibility and synchronization. Meanwhile, Fibre Channel focuses on high-speed data transfer in storage area networks, enabling efficient communication between storage devices and servers. Together, these technologies drive advancements in optical networking.</p></p>
