<p><p>The EV drive system market comprises three primary components: motors, controllers, and transmissions. Motors convert electrical energy into mechanical power, driving the vehicle's wheels. Controllers manage power flow from the battery to the motor, optimizing performance and efficiency. Transmissions facilitate the transfer of power from the motor to the wheels, adapting to varying speeds and loads. Together, these components determine the performance, efficiency, and overall driving experience of electric vehicles, driving innovations and advancements in the market.</p></p>
