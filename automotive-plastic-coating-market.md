<p><ul><li>Primer</li><li>Base Coat</li><li>Clearcoat</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive plastic coating market is divided into three main types: primer, base coat, and clearcoat. Primer serves as an initial layer to enhance adhesion and protect surfaces from corrosion. The base coat provides color and aesthetic appeal, forming the primary protective layer. Clearcoat is the finishing layer that adds gloss, depth, and UV protection, ensuring durability and resilience against environmental factors. Together, these coatings enhance the appearance and longevity of vehicles while protecting plastic components from wear and damage.</p></p>
