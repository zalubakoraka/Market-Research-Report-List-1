<p><ul><li>Mechanical Type</li><li>Hydraulic Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fine blanking machine market is primarily divided into mechanical and hydraulic types. Mechanical fine blanking machines utilize a fixed punch and die setup, providing high-speed operations suitable for mass production with precise tolerances. In contrast, hydraulic fine blanking machines employ hydraulic pressure to facilitate the blanking process, offering greater flexibility and adaptability for complex shapes and thicker materials. Each type caters to different industrial needs, with mechanical machines favored for speed and hydraulic machines preferred for versatility.</p></p>
