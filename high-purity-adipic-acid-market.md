<p><p>The High Purity Adipic Acid market is primarily categorized into three production processes: Cyclohexane Oxidation, Cyclohexene Oxidation, and Phenol Hydrogenation. Cyclohexane Oxidation involves the catalytic oxidation of cyclohexane to produce adipic acid directly, offering high yields and efficiency. Cyclohexene Oxidation uses cyclohexene as a feedstock, providing a more selective route to adipic acid. Phenol Hydrogenation transforms phenol into adipic acid through hydrogenation, yielding a versatile method that can accommodate various feedstock sources and potentially lower environmental impacts.</p></p>
