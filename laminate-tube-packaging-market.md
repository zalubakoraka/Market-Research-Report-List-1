<p><strong>The Laminate Tube Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ABL</li><li>PBL</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Laminate tube packaging is categorized mainly into two types: ABL (Aluminium Barrier Laminate) and PBL (Plastic Barrier Laminate). ABL tubes are made with a layer of aluminum, providing superior protection against light, moisture, and oxygen, making them ideal for products requiring extended shelf life, like pharmaceuticals and cosmetics. Conversely, PBL tubes are composed of plastic layers, offering a lightweight and flexible option, suitable for products that need moderate barrier properties, such as creams and gels. Both types cater to diverse packaging needs.</p></p>
