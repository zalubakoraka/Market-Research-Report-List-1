<p><p>The Polymer Additive Manufacturing market encompasses various machine types, including Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM), which extrudes thermoplastic filaments; Stereolithography (SLA), which utilizes UV light to cure resin; Digital Light Processing (DLP), which projects light to solidify resin layers quickly; Material Jetting (MJ), which deposits droplets of material layer by layer; and Selective Laser Sintering (SLS), which uses lasers to fuse powdered polymers. Other market types may include Binder Jetting and PolyJet technologies, expanding the capabilities of polymer 3D printing.</p></p>
