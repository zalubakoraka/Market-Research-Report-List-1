<p><p>The surgical protective face masks market primarily consists of two types: tie-on masks and earloop masks. Tie-on masks feature long straps that are tied behind the head, providing a secure fit and are often preferred in settings where a snug seal is essential. Earloop masks, on the other hand, have elastic bands that loop around the ears for quick and easy wear, offering convenience and comfort, making them popular in various healthcare settings. Both types are crucial for infection control and safety.</p></p>
