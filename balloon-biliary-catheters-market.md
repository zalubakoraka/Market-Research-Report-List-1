<p><strong>The Balloon Biliary Catheters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Normal Balloon Catheter</li><li>Cutting Balloon Catheter</li><li>Scoring Balloon Catheter</li><li>Drug Eluting Balloon Catheter</li><li>Stent Graft Balloon Catheter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Balloon biliary catheters come in various types, each serving specific therapeutic purposes in managing biliary obstructions. Normal balloon catheters are used for dilation of strictures, while cutting balloon catheters feature small blades for precise tissue incisions. Scoring balloon catheters have surface scoring to facilitate lumen expansion. Drug-eluting balloon catheters release medication to prevent restenosis, and stent graft balloon catheters are designed for deploying stents while simultaneously expanding them. Each type enhances procedural efficacy in biliary interventions.</p></p>
