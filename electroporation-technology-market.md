Consumables include accessories like plates, cuvettes, and electrodes that are necessary for conducting electroporation experiments. Reagents encompass solutions and materials used to enhance the electroporation process, such as buffers and DNA or RNA preparations. Together, these categories support various applications in research, therapy, and biotechnology.</p></p>
